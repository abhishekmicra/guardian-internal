Principal Engineer, Special Projects

Engineering – Full Stack / Full-Time Remote

Gaurdian are looking for an Principal Engineer.

  • Excellent time management and communication skills
  • Ability to have a good sense of humour

  • Experience at a web design agency is ideal but not necessary
